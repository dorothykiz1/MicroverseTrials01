Barack Obama

The first black American President

Obama has demonstrated a remarkable ability to communicate effectively and persuasively, often using his words to inspire and motivate others

  • Barack Obama was born on August 4, 1961, in Honolulu, Hawaii. His mother, Ann Dunham, was a white American from Kansas, and his father, Barack Obama Sr., was a black Kenyan studying in the United States.
  • Obama spent his early childhood in Hawaii, where he attended a private school and later a public school. When he was ten years old, his mother remarried and the family moved to Indonesia, where Obama attended a local school and became fluent in Bahasa Indonesia.
